Learn how the tampon tax affects different people

The tampon tax affects all women, regardless of socioeconomic background. However, some women are unable to afford menstrual products. In a 2017-2018 study of low income women in Missouri, the results showed that 2/3 could not afford menstrual products.



In this game, obstacles are continuously presented, and players must use their up arrow to jump over them. The obstacle increases in height in correspondence to the price accosiated with it. The game acts like a simulation to reflect the difficulties women face in attempting to pay for products.
